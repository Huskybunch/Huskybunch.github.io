D/D/1 System

Deterministic arrivals and departures with one depatrue channel(D/D/1 queue)

确定性到达和离港，只有一个离港通道

D/D/1 queue lends itselt to a graphical or mathermatical solution.

D/D/1队列适合图形化或数学化的解决方案。

什么时候有100辆到达？100辆离开？

最长队伍，和最长delay，整个系统的delaytime

  • When the arrical curve is above the departure curve, a queue will exist当到达曲线在出发曲线之上时，将存在一个队列

  • The point at which the arrival curve meets the departure curve is the moment when the queue dissipates(no more queue exists)到达曲线与离开曲线相遇的点是队列消散的时刻(不再有队列存在)。

  • The point of queue dissipation can be determined by equating appropriate arrival and departure equations, that is通过求解合适的到达和离开方程，可以确定队列的消散点，即

    • 160 + 2(t - 20) = 4t
    • Solving for t gives t = 60

  • Thus the queue that began to form at 8:00A.M. will dissipate 60 minutes later(9:00 a.m.) at which time 240 vehicles will have arrived and departed

  • Individual vehicle delay:

    • Under FIFO queuing discipline, the delay of an individual vehicle is gicen by the horizontal distance水平距离 between arrival and departure curves
    • So，the longest delay is 20 minutes

  • The total length of the queue is given by the vertical distance between arrival and departure curves at that time

    • The longest queue(longest vertical distance between arrical and departure curves) will occur at t = 20 minutes and is 80 vehicles long

  • Total vehicle delay,defined as the summation of the delays of each individual vehicle, is between arrival and departure curves车辆总延误，定义为每辆单独车辆延误的总和，在到达和离开曲线之间

  • In this example, the areas between arrival and departure curves can be determined by summing triangular areas, giving total delay, Dt,as在这个例子中，到达和离开曲线之间的面积可以通过三角形面积的总和来确定，给出总延迟Dt为

  • Because 240 vehicles encounter queuing-delay(as previously determined), the average delay per vehicle is 10 minutes(2400 veh-min/240veh) and the average queue length is 40 vehicles(2400 veh-min/60min)由于240辆车遇到排队延迟(如前所述)，平均每辆车的延迟为10分钟(2400 veh-min/240veh)，平均排队长度为40辆车(2400 veh-min/60min)
