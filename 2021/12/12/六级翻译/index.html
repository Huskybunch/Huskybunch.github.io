四六级翻译必考点

基本步骤 找主干，添枝加叶，检查回读

  1. 谁 是 什么 2 谁 做 什么 3 什么 被 做

之一 one of 名词复数

水浒传是中国文学四大经典小说之一

Water Margin is novel

Water Margin is one of the four classical novels in Chinese literature

明朝统治中国276年，被人们描绘成人类历史上治理有序、社会稳定的最伟大的时代之一

Ming dynasty is described as one of times.

  • 修饰成分有动词用定语从句

Ming dynasty, which has ruled China for 276 years, is described as one of the greatest times(periods) in the humans history.

治理有序、社会稳定

Ming dynasty, which ruled China for 276 years, is described as one of the greatest times(periods) with steady society and ordely governance in the humans history.

越来越

  • A growing number of
  • An increasing number of
  • Increasing + 形容词

越来越多的外国读者也感到这部小说里的故事生动感人、趣味盎然

A growing number of foreign readers also find the story in this novel vivid and intersting.

三个以上的并列： A, B and C

随着中国经济的快速增长和全球影响力的增强，越来越多其他国家的人也开始学习汉语

With the rapid development of Chinese economy and improvement of golbal influence, a growing number of people from other countries started to learn Chinese too.

…的前后都是名词

A of B : B的A

第一节课作业

近年来，中国越来越多的博物馆免费向公众开放。

In recent years, a growing number of museums in China has opened to public for free.

修改： In recent years, a growing number of museums in China are opened to the public free of charge.

如今，展览形式越来越多样

Nowadays, there are a growing forms of exhibition.

Nowadays, there are more and more different forms of exhibition.

越来越多的人也在假日乘高铁外出旅游

A growing number of people alse travel by high-speed rail on vocation.

More and more people also travel by high-speed rail on holidays.

无主句

如果没有 主语

  1. 添加主语 2. 换成被动 3. there be

千百年来，创作了许多诗歌和绘画赞美牡丹

添加主语 we they people

Over thousands of years, Chinese people have created many poetry and drawing to praise penoy.

Over thousands of years, a large number of poetry and drawings have been created (….了 完成时态) to praise peony.

Over thousands of years, there are a large number of poetry and drawings that have been created to praise peony.

普通大众也都喜欢梅花，春节期间常用于家庭装饰。

Common people also like plum blossom, people usually use it to decorate house during Spring Festival.

Common people also like plum blossom, it is often used to decorate house during Spring Festival.

方言被认为是当地文化的一个组成部分。

Dialects are considered as a components of local culturals.

People consider dialects as a components of local culturals.

原因从句

As since+句子

Due to + 短语

但由于汉字有统一的书写，他们交流起来几乎没有任何困难

But since Chinese characters are written in a unify way, they hardly have any difficulty in communicating.

  • have difficulty in doing sth.

可以预见，随着运动设施不断改善，愈来愈多的人将会去体育馆健身。

It can be excepted that as the constant improvement of sport facilities, a growing number of people will go to the gym to work out./exercise

有

  1. have\has 拥有

I have money.

  1. there are/is/were/was/have been/will be 存在

There are 2 people in the classroom

  1. with 伴随

With this car, I will no longer be late.

作为中国的国宝，武术有上百种不同的风格，是世界上练的最多的武术形式。

As the national treasure, Chinese martial arts have hundreds of different styles and are the most practiced form of martial arts in the word.

华山过去很少有人光临，因为上山的道路极其危险

There were few people visited Mount Hua, because/as the road up the mountain is extremely dangeous.

他们可以乘飞机到达所有大城市，还有很多城市也在筹建机场。

They can fly to arrive all cities, there are many cities, which are planning to build airpods.

时间

Over, for +时间段

For 4 years,

Over the past four years, Have /has/had

自从——————————–时间点

Since last month +完成时态

数百年来，当地人沿着河边建起了住宅和集市(了完成时态)

For hundreds of years, local people/locals have built houses and markets along the river bank.

自上世纪九十年代安装缆车以来，参观人数大大增加

Since the installation of cable cars in 1990s, the number of visitors has greatly increased.

  • 在过去几十年 For the past decades
  • 采取措施做某事 take measures to do sth

中国武术的起源可以追溯到自卫的需要

The origin of Chinese martial art can trace back to need of self-defence

利用风来发电可以追溯到1980年

Using wind to generate electricity goes back to 1890s.

故事追溯到十九世纪中叶。

The story goes back to the middle of 19th century.

磁铁用于航海，至少可以追溯到11世纪

Using magnent in navigation goes back to at least 11th century

  • 2nd

潍坊以“风筝之都”而闻名，已有将近2400年放飞风筝的历史。

Weifang is known as “Kite Capital”, and has a history of flying kites for nearly 2400 years.

它风景独特，尤以其日出和云海著称

It has a unique view, and is especially known for its sunrise and sea of cloud.

Wuzhen, an ancient water town in Zhejiang Province, is located along the reiver bank of Beijing-Hangzhou grand canal
