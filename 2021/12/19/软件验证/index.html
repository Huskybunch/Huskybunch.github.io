软件验证

发表于 2021-12-19 分类于 软件验证

软件验证期末考试复习，主要以提纲为主

Note

There may be some questions about Design by Contract. You would not be asked to write some program codes, but you may be asked to explain some concepts/features of DbC.可能会有一些关于契约式设计的问题。你不会被要求写一些程序代码，但是你可能会被要求解释DbC的一些概念/特性。

You need to know what propositional and predicate logic are. In addition,the different terminologiesrelated to each one. Also, natural deduction rules to prove the validity of formulas.

你需要知道什么是命题逻辑和谓词逻辑。此外，不同的术语与每一个相关。同时，用自然演绎规则来证明公式的有效性。

There would not be any questions about Coq.

There would be some questions about Models and concepts around models. For example, satisfiable, valid, unsatisfiable, truth table, soundness, completeness, and equisatisfiable.

会有一些关于模型和模型周围的概念的问题。例如，可满足的、有效的、不可满足的、真值表、健全性、完备性和相等可满足的。

There may be some questions about Conjunctive Normal Form (CNF) and converting a formula to CNF. You can use any of the approaches described in the slides to convert a formula to CNF.

在连词标准形式和将公式转换成连词标准形式方面可能存在一些问题。您可以使用幻灯片中描述的任何方法将公式转换为CNF

There may be some questions about SAT and SMT solvers concepts. You would not be asked to write Z3 codes

关于SAT和SMT求解器的概念可能存在一些问题。你不会被要求写Z3代码

There would be some questions to verify some pieces of codes with Hoare logic.

用Hoare逻辑来验证一些代码会有一些问题。

There would be some questions about Dafny. In the questions, you will be asked to provide precondition, postcondition, loop invariant (and loop termination), for some pieces of codes. The codes may or may not include arrays, recursive methods, and/or classes.

会有一些关于达芙妮的问题。在问题中，你会被要求为一些代码提供前置条件、后置条件、循环不变量(和循环终止)。这些代码可能包含也可能不包含数组、递归方法和/或类。

Regarding Model checking (temporal logic), you may be asked for some of the terminologies or concepts. You would not be asked to write any codes in Promela (SPIN).

关于模型检查(时间逻辑)，您可能会被问到一些术语或概念。您不会被要求在Promela (SPIN)中编写任何代码。
